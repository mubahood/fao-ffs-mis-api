## Privacy Policy

### Introduction

The Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) FFS-MIS VSLA mobile application ("the App") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of Village Savings and Loan Association (VSLA) groups and their members in Karamoja, Uganda. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

### Information We Collect

#### 1. Personal Information

When you register as a VSLA group administrator, secretary, treasurer, or member, we collect:

**Required Information:**
- Full name
- Mobile phone number
- Country of residence

**Optional Information:**
- Email address
- Physical address
- District, subcounty, parish, and village

#### 2. VSLA Group Information

We collect information about your VSLA group:
- Group name and description
- Establishment date
- Meeting frequency and location
- Estimated number of members
- Group structure (administrators, officers)

#### 3. Savings Cycle Information

We collect information about your VSLA savings cycles:
- Cycle name and duration
- Share values and contribution amounts
- Loan interest rates and terms
- Meeting schedules

#### 4. Financial Transaction Data

We record:
- Member savings contributions
- Loan disbursements and repayments
- Share purchases
- Interest calculations
- Account balances

#### 5. Automatically Collected Information

- Device information (model, OS version)
- App usage statistics
- Location data (with your permission)
- Crash reports and error logs

### How We Use Your Information

#### 1. Primary Purposes

**Group Management:**
- Enable VSLA group formation and administration
- Track membership and roles
- Facilitate communication between members

**Financial Management:**
- Record savings and loans
- Calculate interest and dividends
- Generate financial reports
- Track account balances

**Reporting and Monitoring:**
- Provide dashboards for group administrators
- Generate reports for FAO and implementing partners
- Monitor group performance and health

#### 2. Communication

- Send SMS notifications about meetings
- Alert members about loan payments
- Share important updates and announcements
- Deliver login credentials to officers

#### 3. System Improvement

- Analyze usage patterns to improve the App
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Develop new features based on user needs

### Data Sharing and Disclosure

#### We Share Your Information With:

**1. Your VSLA Group:**
- Group administrators can view member information
- Officers (secretary, treasurer) have access to financial records
- Members can view their own account details

**2. FAO and Implementing Partners:**
- Aggregated, anonymized data for program monitoring
- Reports on VSLA activities in Karamoja
- Performance metrics and impact assessments

**3. Service Providers:**
- SMS gateway providers (for notifications)
- Cloud hosting services (for data storage)
- Payment processors (for mobile money transactions)

#### We Do NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data for marketing purposes
- Disclose individual financial details publicly

### Data Security

#### We Protect Your Information By:

**Technical Measures:**
- Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
- Secure server infrastructure
- Regular security audits
- Backup and disaster recovery systems

**Administrative Measures:**
- Role-based access controls
- Staff training on data protection
- Confidentiality agreements
- Incident response procedures

**Physical Measures:**
- Secure data center facilities
- Controlled access to servers
- Environmental safeguards

### Your Rights

#### You Have the Right To:

**Access:**
- View your personal information
- Download your transaction history
- Request copies of your data

**Correction:**
- Update your profile information
- Correct inaccurate records
- Modify your contact details

**Deletion:**
- Request account deletion (subject to legal requirements)
- Remove optional information
- Opt out of non-essential communications

**Portability:**
- Export your financial records
- Transfer data to another platform (where feasible)

**Objection:**
- Decline optional data collection
- Opt out of analytics tracking
- Refuse location services

### Data Retention

**Active Accounts:**
- We retain your data as long as your account is active
- Financial records kept for the duration of each savings cycle

**Inactive Accounts:**
- Accounts inactive for 2 years may be archived
- Financial records retained for 7 years (legal requirement)

**Deleted Accounts:**
- Personal information deleted within 90 days
- Anonymous transaction records retained for reporting

### Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via:
- In-app notifications
- SMS alerts
- Email (if provided)

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

### VSLA Group Management

#### Responsibilities of Group Administrators:

**Registration:**
- Provide truthful information about the group
- Register legitimate officers
- Ensure member consent before adding them
- Maintain accurate membership records

**Financial Management:**
- Record transactions accurately and promptly
- Safeguard group funds
- Follow agreed-upon loan policies
- Generate timely reports for members

**Meetings:**
- Conduct regular meetings as scheduled
- Maintain quorum requirements
- Keep accurate minutes
- Inform members of decisions

#### Responsibilities of Group Officers:

**Secretary:**
- Maintain accurate records
- Communicate with members
- Prepare meeting agendas
- Document group decisions

**Treasurer:**
- Record all financial transactions
- Safeguard group funds
- Provide financial reports
- Ensure transparency

### Savings Cycle Rules

#### You Agree That:
- Only one active savings cycle is allowed per group at a time
- Cycle start and end dates must be clearly defined
- Share values and loan terms are binding once set
- Interest rates must comply with Ugandan law
- All members must agree to cycle terms

#### Financial Transactions:
- All contributions must be recorded in the App
- Loan disbursements require proper approval
- Interest calculations follow configured settings
- Late payment penalties apply as agreed
- Dividends distributed according to shares
